Web design

I was the design lead for several websites within the Magix and VEGAS brands, namely their official websites magix.com and vegascreativesoftware.com respectively. As a member of the web development team, I developed concepts and designs for new website elements and helped implement these to improve usability for customers or boost conversion rates, for example.

Order boxes

Main navigation for magix.com

Magix is a software company which offers a wide variety of products for audio, video and photo editing. Since the product selection is so vast, it was essential to create a navigation that offered users a comprehensible overview and enabled them to quickly find what they were looking for.

Order boxes

The VEGAS brand provides professional video editing software. The brand's main focus is the software VEGAS Pro, which is sold in three packages at different price points according to users' needs. To allow users to comfortably compare the prices of subscriptions, perpetual versions and upgrades of the different packages, I developed new order boxes.
The goal was to enable users to compare prices at a glance and to make the various payment plans easy to understand.
