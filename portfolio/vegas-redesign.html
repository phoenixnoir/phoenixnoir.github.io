VEGAS brand redesign

VEGAS, a well-established brand in the video editing market for over 20 years, underwent a rebrand in 2021 to attract a wider, younger audience. The new brand identity incorporated vibrant colours, a playful typeface, and more meaningful logos and icons.

Colours & Logos

VEGAS software is offered in three packages: VEGAS Edit, VEGAS Pro, and VEGAS Post. Each package includes "VEGAS Pro," with the Pro and Post versions containing additional software like Sound Forge for music editing and Effects for video special effects. Distinct colours and icons were used to represent each software within the packages.
